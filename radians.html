Radian Mode



Instructions


Objective: Sit down in a chairs representing angles on the unit circle before other players.

Set-up: Place 16 chairs in a circle each representing an angle in radians on the unit circle.

Game Play: When the cards are flipped, players race to any of the seats shown on the cards and sit down. A round ends when all seats are taken. The person without a seat is eliminated. The remaining players compete with one less card. The fastest player wins the match.
